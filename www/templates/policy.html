Safe Place Policy

Dallas Fort Work (September 2015)

Dallas Fort Work is dedicated to providing a respectful experience for everyone. All Dallas Fort Work events, operations, staff, members and guests agree to follow a Safe Place Policy that informs how we interact with each other in the coworking space, and seeks to prevent harassment, abuse and disruptive hostility.

We actively reject harassment. Dallas Fort Work holds itself to the “Pledge of Neutrality” and holds our staff, members, and guests to the agreement below with the goal of creating a welcoming work and social environment. People violating these rules may be expelled from the space or an event.

We pledge to refrain from:

Verbal

  • Deliberate verbal or physical intimidation;
  • Offensive verbal comments;
  • Hate speech or disparagement against any protected class including (race, color, religion, native origin, age, sex, sexual orientation, parenthood status, citizenship, familial status, disability status, veteran status, occupation or appearance);
  • Hostile discouragement or disparagement;

Sexual

  • Unwelcome sexual attention;
  • Unwelcome physical contact;

Physical Space

  • Stalking, following and undesired accompanying;
  • Entering or occupying private spaces without permission;
  • Disruption of meetings, business transactions, talks or events;
  • Destruction or theft of property.
  • Photography or recording without consent;

Contact Oren Salomon (Owner) with any issues that arise, email: oren@dallasfortwork.com
